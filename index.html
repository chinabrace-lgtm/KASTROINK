About KASTROINK

stablished in 2014, Kastroink Pty (Ltd) is a privately incorporated company in accordance with the provisions of the Companies Act of 2008, as amended. Founded by a team of visionary proprietors following thorough research and consultation, Kastroink has built a strong reputation for reliability, innovation, and excellence in service delivery. Kastroink is well-positioned to manage a wide range of projects, leveraging its ability to secure essential resources and engage highly skilled professionals. The company’s core operations span building construction, renovations, maintenance, and interior design, offering tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of both corporate and individual clients.

Guided by the principles of Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment (BBBEE), Kastroink's strategic approach is grounded in innovative investment strategies and a strong commitment to economic empowerment. The company’s diversified investment portfolios and service offerings enable it to deliver impactful, client- focused outcomes across sectors. Kastroink is also deeply committed to community development through its in-house foundation, reflecting its belief in sustainable growth and social responsibility. By understanding the needs and aspirations of its clients and communities, Kastroink strives to create value-driven, long-term partnerships.Recognised for its versatility and effectiveness, Kastroink Pty (Ltd) continues to stand out as a dynamic leader in its industry

Our Services

General Building Construction

We undertake a wide range of building projects, delivering durable and high-quality structures for residential, commercial, and industrial purposes. Our team ensures every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards

Civil Engineering Construction

Our civil engineering services cover infrastructure development such as roads, drainage systems, and structural foundations. We apply industry best practices and innovative solutions to deliver safe and efficient civil works.

Building Renovations and maintenance

We provide expert renovation and maintenance services to enhance and preserve the condition of buildings. Whether it’s structural upgrades or routine upkeep, we ensure properties remain functional, safe, and aesthetically pleasing.

Electrical Maintenance

Our skilled technicians offer reliable electrical maintenance services, including fault finding, repairs, and installations. We ensure all systems comply with safety regulations and operate efficiently.

Fencing

We install a variety of fencing solutions to meet security and aesthetic requirements for residential, commercial, and industrial sites. Our offerings include palisade, mesh, and custom-designed fences.

Material Supply and Delivery

We supply and deliver high-quality building materials promptly to your site, helping you stay on schedule and meet project goals without delays.

Disinfecting

Our disinfecting services ensure clean and hygienic environments for homes, offices, and public facilities. We use professional-grade products and techniques to eliminate harmful pathogens and promote health and safety
